# Ratio (Price / SMA50)

## Definition

This indicator calculates the ratio of the closing price to the 50-period Simple Moving Average (SMA). It measures how far the price has extended away from its medium-term average.

## Mathematical Equation

$$
\text{Ratio} = \frac{\text{Close}}{SMA_{50}}
$$

## Trading Significance

1.  **Extension**: High values (significantly > 1) indicate the price is overextended to the upside. Low values (< 1) indicate it is overextended to the downside.

2.  **Mean Reversion**: Extreme ratios often precede a reversion to the mean (the moving average).
