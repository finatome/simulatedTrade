Candlestick Patterns¶

Candlestick patterns are visual representations of price movements that can signal potential future market behavior. They are formed by one or more candlesticks and are used by traders to identify trading opportunities.

Single Candle Patterns¶

Pattern Type Description
Hammer Bullish Small body at top, long lower shadow. Reversal at bottom of downtrend.
Inverted Hammer Bullish Small body at bottom, long upper shadow. Reversal at bottom of downtrend.
Hanging Man Bearish Small body at top, long lower shadow. Reversal at top of uptrend.
Shooting Star Bearish Small body at bottom, long upper shadow. Reversal at top of uptrend.
Doji Indecision Open approx. equal to Close. Market neutrality.
Spinning Top Indecision Small body centered between long shadows. Consolidation.
Marubozu Continuation Long body with no shadows. Strong directional conviction.

Dual Candle Patterns¶

Pattern Type Description
Bullish Engulfing Bullish Large green candle engulfs previous small red candle.
Bearish Engulfing Bearish Large red candle engulfs previous small green candle.
Piercing Line Bullish Green candle closes > 50% into previous red candle.
Dark Cloud Cover Bearish Red candle closes > 50% into previous green candle.
Bullish Harami Bullish Small green candle inside previous large red candle.
Bearish Harami Bearish Small red candle inside previous large green candle.
Tweezer Bottoms Bullish Matching lows. Support indication.
Tweezer Tops Bearish Matching highs. Resistance indication.

Triple Candle Patterns¶

Pattern Type Description
Morning Star Bullish Red, Gap Down Star, Gap Up Green. Top reversal.
Evening Star Bearish Green, Gap Up Star, Gap Down Red. Bottom reversal.
Three White Soldiers Bullish Three consecutive long green candles.
Three Black Crows Bearish Three consecutive long red candles.
Three Inside Up Bullish Bullish Harami + Confirmation.
Three Inside Down Bearish Bearish Harami + Confirmation.
Three Outside Up Bullish Bullish Engulfing + Confirmation.
Three Outside Down Bearish Bearish Engulfing + Confirmation.

Complex & Continuation Patterns¶

Pattern Type Description
Rising Three Methods Continuation Long Green, 3 Small Red, Long Green. Bullish pause.
Falling Three Methods Continuation Long Red, 3 Small Green, Long Red. Bearish pause.
